Welcome!

Below, you'll find a variety of classic arcade games! I hope you enjoy!

Snake

Snake is a video game where the player maneuvers a line which grows in length, with the line itself being a primary obstacle. Points are earned by eating the apple that randomly appears on the screen.

Pong

In pong, players use the paddles to hit a ball back and forth. The first player to reach a score of 3 wins. A player's score is incremented when 5 points are earned. A player obtains a point when the other player fails to return the ball.

Dodge

In dodge, the player can earn points by avoiding all the obstacles that fall from the top of the screen. Beware of the zapper on the left-hand side!

Memory Tiles

Four colored buttons light up in a specific pattern. After displaying the pattern, the player must repeat the pattern by clicking the buttons in proper order. The pattern gets longer each time the player completes the pattern and initially starts at a length of 3. The game ends once the player runs out of tries.
